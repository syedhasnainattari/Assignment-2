About this template

A student registration form collects all the necessary information to register students for a course. No matter what subject you teach, this free Student Registration Form allows students to quickly and easily sign up for your class online. Simply customize the form to meet your needs, publish it on your school’s website, and watch the registrations roll in! Students can provide their contact information, detail their academic history, list extracurriculars and additional interests, and even pay registration fees if required. Submissions will be sent to your secure Jotform account, easily accessible on any device — even offline.
